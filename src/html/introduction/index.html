QC TOOL

As NBCUniversal continues to expand our portfolio there is a growing number of digital tools needed to maintain the distribution of our content. We were approached by the GMO group to create two different UI’s that will aid the SKY acquisition. The “International Dashboard” and the “Spot Check & Mark Up” view. Eventually these tools will be leveraged in a few systems outside of SKY as well.

Contents

  • The QC Tool
  • The Problem
  • Current Workflow

QC Tool

We are building the International Dashboard to allow users to easily search and filter their orders to see the most recent updates. The dashboard will provide visibility of important metadata such as the “Go Live” data, brand owner, processing status, and at risk warnings. Powerful filters will be available for the users to quickly locate a specific order and see the latest status.

The Problem

Currently there is no software that allows SKY and NBCUniversal users to track the progress of their orders. Orders are placed in an external system called Landmark, they are processed in Mediator, and then the videos are ready for playout. Without a system to track the orders and their progress states it is very difficult to maintain the large amount of content that needs to be packaged and delivered properly for our international viewers.

Workflow

1

International partners order our content in Landmark

2

Order are processed through Mediator

3

Mediator conducts an Auto QC

4

NBCU technicians perform a manual QC; If needed makes fixes

5

One processing is complete, content is ready at the platform and sent to our partners
