International & TACT Dashboards

As NBCUniversal continues to expand our portfolio there is a growing number of digital tools needed to maintain the distribution of our content. We were approached by the GMO group to create two different UI’s that will aid the SKY acquisition. The “International Dashboard” and the “TACT Dashboard/QC Tool”. Eventually these products will be leveraged in a few systems outside of SKY as well.

The Problem

Currently there is no software that allows SKY and internal users to track the progress of orders we send to them. Orders are placed in an external system called Landmark, they are processed in Mediator, and then the videos are ready for playout. Without a system to track the orders and their progress states it is difficult to maintain the large amount of content that needs to be packaged and delivered properly for our international viewers.

There is also no internal system for technicians to conduct a full QC on the content we send to SKY. Users currently use the “Spot Check & Mark Up” feature in Mediator as a QC tool. Although the workflow in Mediator works, it doesn’t allow users to easily flex the data and the UI is clunky/outdated.

International Dashboard

We are building the International Dashboard to allow users to easily search and filter their orders to see the most recent updates. A pipeline of the progress will be illustrated in each order to quickly and easily communicate its status. The dashboard will also provide visibility of important metadata such as the “Go Live” data, brand owner, processing status, and at risk warnings. Powerful filters will be available for the users to quickly locate a specific order and see the latest status.

TACT Dashboard/QC Tool

The TACT Dashboard orders will be synchronous with the International Dashboard but with a different purpose- internal technicians will use it to open the QC tool for that order. The QC tool allows user to segment the video, add comments, and access the error log. Creating our own allows us to upgrade the old & clunky Mediator UI into a seamless design that will create a better user experience and allow users to get their work done faster. This spot check is the “Manual review” pipeline step. The QC tool will also contain a commenting feature so users can identify timecodes in the video that need attention. There will be an error log modal so users can check and track them.

Current Workflow

1

International partners order our content in Landmark

2

Order are processed through Mediator

3

Mediator conducts an Auto QC

4

NBCU technicians perform a manual QC; If needed makes fixes

5

One processing is complete, content is ready at the platform and sent to our partners
